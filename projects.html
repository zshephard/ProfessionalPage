Photogrammetry

3D Model Construction

For my photogrammetry workflow I use an open source program called WebODM. Below is a 3D model I constructed of a lint roller using only my phone camera and WebODM. For best viewing, in the window below check the "Show Model" box under the "Textured Model" tab in the menu to the left.

Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)

I own a 3DR Solo UAS and currently have it equipt with a GoPro and gimbal. I am using the free apps Mission Planner and Tower to plan autonomous flights in order to capture photogrammetry data. Along with WebODM, I am in the process of making Digital Surface Models to demonstrate the use of UAS and geomorphic change detection. Projects soon to be posted.
